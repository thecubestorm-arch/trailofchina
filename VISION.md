# Trail of China — Vision & Mission

## Why We Exist

China is intimidating for Western tourists. The language barrier, the apps, the censorship, the payment systems, the train booking — everything is different. There is no single trusted, beautiful, practical English-language resource that says: "Here's exactly what you need, step by step."

**The Problem:** Westerners Google "China travel" and find:
- **Lonely Planet** → superficial, outdated
- **TripAdvisor** → reviews, not guides
- **The China Guide** → SEO spam, no trust
- **Reddit** → chaotic, contradictory

**Our Promise:** *"Travel China Without Confusion"* — everything you need, from someone who's been 4 times, speaks Mandarin, has family in Beijing and Shanghai. No bullshit, no fear-mongering, just practical help.

## Design Principles

- **Warm, not cold** — terracotta/cream/teal, NOT corporate red/blue
- **Editorial, not wiki** — magazine layouts, not card grids
- **Trustworthy, not flashy** — subtle Chinese elements (watermarks), not kitsch
- **Practical, not academic** — "Here's what to do", not "China has a rich history..."
- **Safe, not scary** — China IS safe, we don't peddle fear
